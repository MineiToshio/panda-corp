# /pandacorp:new-idea

Crystallizes an idea as a card in the ideas base. The idea can come from `$ARGUMENTS`, from a specific description, or from **a whole discovery conversation** (e.g. after `/pandacorp:explore`).

## Steps

1. **Synthesize from the whole conversation.** Don't stop at just `$ARGUMENTS` or the last message: go back through **the whole** conversation and reconstruct the idea with what was said, pushed back on and concluded. If the idea comes from `/pandacorp:explore`, also read its draft `factory/ideas/_drafts/<slug>.md` if it exists (it concentrates the exploration thread). Rules depending on what you find:
   - If the conversation explored **several** candidate ideas, list each one on a line and confirm with the owner which one(s) to capture (you can create one card per idea).
   - If there is only a short description and little context, ask at most 2-3 questions for the essentials that are missing (what exactly hurts? is it for them or to sell? what do they imagine as the solution?). If it's already enough, don't ask.
2. **Locate the factory**: the ideas base is in `factory/ideas/`, at the factory root (the repo where this skill runs). Read `_idea-template.md` for the exact format.
3. **Light research** (delegate to the `researcher` agent, a quick pass): name the main **similares** / competitors + our **differential / wedge**; evidence of the pain in others; obvious feasibility; and the conviction inputs — **why now**, **founder-fit** (does an asset/channel of the owner apply?) and the **#1 risk**. Also check the existing cards **including the `status: discarded` ones** (the Descartado column = the single do-not-recommend memory): if this idea matches one already **discarded** — or fits a recurring **rejection pattern** (the profile anti-patterns / past discard reasons) — do NOT silently proceed: surface it to the owner ("ya descartaste esto / sueles rechazar esto por X; ¿lo capturo igual?") and only continue if he confirms. Read what the owner already has (`factory/portfolio.md` + existing project folders) to judge **standalone vs fold-in** into an app he already owns, and a **market reality check** for the owner's country (does the revenue rail —affiliate/payment/ads— and the customer base actually exist and pay from e.g. Perú, or does the premise assume US/abroad infra?). Reuse the conversation's evidence if it already has it. This is not the deep research — that comes in the product phase.
4. **Score (0-100)** (read `factory/profile.md` to weigh return and fit): real and frequent need/pain (30%), ease of implementation with golden paths or as tooling/prompt/automation (25%), **return or value to the owner** —monetary, of opportunity (reach/network/positioning/learning) or personal, weighted by the profile's appetite— (25%), **fit and advantage** —alignment with the profile + differentiation— (20%). Then assign an honest **verdict** — **build** / **validate** (a key unknown → a concrete 7-day test) / **integrate** (it's really a feature of an app he already owns → name it in `fold_into`) — plus the **#1 risk** that survives an honest red team (don't only sell the upside). Document the rationale in "Evaluation notes".
5. **Create the card** `factory/ideas/<slug-in-english>.md` with full frontmatter incl. the decision fields (`verdict`, `the_bet`, `fold_into`, `why_now`, `kill_risk`, `validation_step`) + `project_type`/`profile_alignment`/`return_type`, `status: discovered`, `created:` today. Write the body per `_idea-template.md` — the **2-layer memo, sell the value not a me-too**: *Veredicto + la apuesta* on top, then *De un vistazo* (Problema as storytelling, diferencial, por qué ahora, por qué tú, riesgo #1, test de 7 días, scorecard) and *Profundizar* (solución, features, similares, comparación y valor, retorno, red team, evidencia, notas). If an exploration draft existed (`factory/ideas/_drafts/<slug>.md`), **delete it** — the card now replaces it.
6. Report to the owner: card summary, score and whether you recommend it or not (with a short rationale).

## Rules
- One card per idea; if a similar one already exists (read the frontmatter), update it instead of duplicating.
- Always classify the card with `project_type` (what type of solution: web, mobile, desktop, ai, claude-code, prompt-system, automation, cli, rework…) and `return_type` (monetary, opportunity, personal or mixed).
- A `personal` or `opportunity` return is also documented with rigor — the score weighs value and opportunity, not just monetization.
- To explore and clarify a fuzzy idea BEFORE capturing it, that's the job of `/pandacorp:explore`; this skill is the crystallization step.
