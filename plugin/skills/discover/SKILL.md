# /pandacorp:discover

Opportunity discovery **that makes the owner dream AND decide** — in **two phases**, so his taste enters BEFORE the spend. **Phase 1** sweeps wide and cheap: 2-3 hunting lenses, researchers mining only **live-verified sources** (`sources.md`, next to this skill: `plugin/skills/discover/sources.md`), output = a numbered sheet of **8-12 one-line teasers**. The owner picks the 2-3 that spark. **Phase 2** deep-dives ONLY the picked ones through the hard gates into **memo-pitch** cards: it sells the value like a pitch to an investor (so a builder says "this is the one") and gives the cold criterio to decide (market, distribution, gaps, risk). `$ARGUMENTS` can narrow the niche or pin a lens (e.g.: "/pandacorp:discover extensiones", "/pandacorp:discover tools for teachers"); without arguments, the run picks the lenses.

**Open scope.** Not limited to "monetizable apps": an opportunity can be a web/mobile app, a browser extension, a Claude Code tool, a prompt system, an automation, a rework. What matters is **a real problem with good return**.

**Return is NOT just money, and the 3 return paths are EQUAL priority (owner rule).** A hit (many users), several micro-apps (~100-200 USD/mo each that add up), and a non-monetizable app that solves the owner's own life all count the same. The hero of the report is chosen by **pain strength + founder-fit + buildability**, NOT by return type.

**Bias to easy-to-build micro-apps (owner rule).** Prefer **micro** (a cotizapdf — one role/one flow/one feature) and **small** ideas. A **large** idea (weeks, 10-15 FRDs) is only proposed when the pain is **highly recurrent, with strong complaints, and strong odds of monetization** — otherwise re-scope it to its core or kill it.

**The job is to DECIDE, not to inform.** The owner reads the memo and knows *this one yes / this one no*. Conviction comes from curation (the weak were killed before he saw them), his own pick (Phase 1 put his taste in the funnel), the hot block that makes him dream, the cold block that answers every doubt (market, distribution, source-of-truth, legal), and an explicit verdict.

## Step 0 — Read the profile + map what the owner already has

Read `factory/profile.md` (interests, hobbies, likes/dislikes, goals, **assets/levers** —audience, community, niche, data—, monetization appetite, project types, the owner's **country/market** — Perú/LATAM, on which the market-reality check depends, and the **attraction/rejection patterns** from past runs). If it doesn't exist, say so and suggest onboarding (you can still run the general lenses).

Then read **what the owner already owns**, for the extension/fold-in check (Step 5): `factory/portfolio.md` + the owner's existing project folders (the profile's `projects_path`, default = the factory's sibling folders). Note each app's name + one-line purpose. **Do NOT freeze their scope (owner rule):** these apps are built outside the factory; the goal is not to *subsume* near-niche ideas into them but to detect when a candidate is better delivered as a **proposed extension** of one of them.

**Build the EXCLUSION SET (do NOT re-recommend).** Read **every existing idea card** `factory/ideas/*.md` in **ANY status** — discovered / recommended / in-pipeline / shipped / **discarded**. The `discarded` cards (the Descartado column) are the **single source of truth for rejected ideas**. For each card, record **the PAIN/PROBLEM it solves + for whom** — NOT just its title or its solution (dedup is by PROBLEM; the operational rule lives in Gate 3.0). Anything whose core pain is already on the board in any status (incl. discarded) is excluded for the whole run (teaser triage + Gate 3.0 + Step 4). Also read the **recent teaser sheets** in `factory/ideas/_drafts/discover-*.md` (last ~3 runs): don't re-tease a candidate the owner already passed on, unless there is genuinely NEW evidence — and then say so.

**Learn from WHY he discarded (rejection patterns).** For each `discarded` card read its `discard_reason` (else the body) + the profile's dislikes/anti-patterns + the reactions recorded on past teaser sheets, and distil the recurring **rejection patterns** (the generalizable reason, e.g. "saturated/strong-incumbent", "B2B sales-heavy", "doesn't leverage my channel", "re-implements an owned Panda* app"). These feed the teaser triage and kill criterion #9.

## Phase 1 — the teaser sweep (wide and cheap)

**1a. Pick 2-3 hunting lenses** (declare them in the sheet; `$ARGUMENTS` can pin one). The old two-stream A/B split is retired — lenses rotate across runs so the funnel doesn't drill the same dry well twice:

| Lens | What it hunts | Primary sources (see `sources.md`) |
|---|---|---|
| **App-enhancement** | Complaints about mainstream apps → browser extensions / companion micro-apps. ONLY tolerant platforms (YouTube UX-mods, Steam, Gmail/GCal, Twitch, Notion). **Anti-picks, hard:** WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Spotify-API companions, YouTube ad-blocking. | CWS 1★ reviews · product subreddits · Google forums · Canny boards |
| **Owner identities** | Pains of the owner's OTHER identities beyond collecting: TikTok creator, frontend+AI dev, Peruvian (SUNAT/SMB), gamer/anime consumer. | niche subreddits · Apple App Store 1-2★ · AlternativeTo · Ask HN |
| **Own-itch / QoL** | The owner's own workflows and frictions; personal-utility apps (monetizable or not). Seeded by the owner's pasted TikTok comment/DM themes when available. | owner-pasted themes · Ask HN QoL asks · autocomplete gaps |
| **Incumbent challenger** | A *successful* SaaS with many paying users, attacked in one of two combinable modes: **(A) AI-first rebuild of ONE workflow** (incumbents bolt AI on; they can't rebuild their core around it) · **(B) 80/20 unbundle** (ship ONLY the feature people pay for, radically simpler/cheaper — the incumbent can't match without cannibalizing its suite pricing). Rules: single-player/prosumer incumbents or one separable workflow — never team-locked B2B suites; the unbundled feature must be a retention/payment driver, not a free commodity; "same + AI button" is NOT a wedge. | incumbent's Capterra 1★ + Canny + AlternativeTo · "simpler alternative to X" demand · Microns ARR |
| Collector niche | **DORMANT** — the owner's board already covers its pains. Wakes only on genuinely NEW signal (e.g. a theme the owner pastes from his audience). | owner-pasted themes only |

**1b. Launch `researcher` agents in parallel (one per lens), evidence-first but TEASER-CHEAP.** Give each: its lens's mining recipes from `sources.md` (only sources marked fetchable; follow the query mechanics), the EXCLUSION SET (pains + titles, any status incl. discarded), the rejection patterns, and the anti-picks. Each brings **4-6 candidates as teasers**: one-line bet (la apuesta) · the single **strongest first-hand evidence link** · the prior workaround (how people solve it today) · a rough complexity guess (micro/small/large) · for monetary ideas, one line on whether the rail works from Perú. NO memos, NO full market studies — that spend is reserved for Phase 2.

**1c. Teaser triage (the cheap gates).** For each candidate: **dedup vs the board** (name the single nearest existing card and the pain each addresses — same core pain in ANY status = dropped, a genuine improvement = noted as an UPDATE to that card, never a second card); **rejection-pattern check** (matches one → dropped, name the pattern); **anti-pick check**; **feasibility sanity** (one person, micro/small band, no heavy regulation — a large survives the triage only with a visibly near-sure monetary case). Target **8-12 survivors**; killed teasers are listed one-line at the sheet's foot ("descartadas del barrido y por qué").

**1d. Present the teaser sheet + wait for the owner's picks.** Spanish, scannable, numbered — per teaser: **la apuesta** (one line) · lens · cubeta estimada · the evidence link · the dedup one-liner. Persist the sheet to `factory/ideas/_drafts/discover-<YYYY-MM-DD>.md` (gitignored Spanish) so the choice survives the session. Ask the owner to pick the **2-3 that spark** (and optionally react to the rest: "meh"/"no" + why). **Unattended/scheduled runs stop here** — the sheet IS the report; Phase 2 runs when the owner returns and picks. **Learn from the response (DR-053):** reasons on passed-over teasers are recorded on the sheet and, when generalizable, distilled into the profile's attraction/rejection patterns. Non-picked teasers do NOT become cards and do NOT enter the exclusion set — but the sheet keeps them so near-future runs don't blindly re-tease them.

## Phase 2 — the deep dive (ONLY the picked)

2. **Deep research per picked candidate — launch `researcher` in parallel, full evidence:**
   - **Prior workaround (The Mom Test — behavior over opinion):** how do people solve this TODAY (a spreadsheet, a manual process, hiring someone)? An existing workaround is the #1 predictor of willingness-to-pay; record it in `prior_workaround`. Prefer evidence of *past behavior* over hypothetical opinion.
   - **Willingness-to-pay oracle (monetary ideas):** check Microns.io (per `sources.md`) — does this category ever sell with real ARR?
   - **Source the pain in the RIGHT market:** when the idea is for the owner's niche, mine the **Spanish/LATAM** buyer's complaints — never mine an Anglo/B2B pain and then staple a Spanish channel on it (a false demand↔distribution match).
   - **Similares** — the real existing products/competitors, named, one line + link each.
   - **Differential / wedge** — concretely why ours is better/different. For challenger ideas the wedge must be **structural** (AI *is* the flow · radical simplicity · price the incumbent can't match · Spanish/LATAM-native), never "same + AI button".
   - **Dream features** — 3-6 capabilities that make it *exciting* (the prize).
   - **Demand signal**, **incumbent strength**, **why now**.
   - **Distribution / founder-fit** — can the owner reach the first 10 customers? His TikTok counts ONLY for ideas aimed at his audience (geek/collector/Steam/YouTube); prosumer/dev tools must name another channel (CWS SEO, Reddit, communities) or admit "none".
   - **Market reality (owner's country) — for MONETARY ideas only (owner rule):** does the revenue rail (payment/affiliate/ads) AND the customer base actually exist and *pay* from Perú/LATAM? (See the verified Perú payments table in `sources.md`.) Name the in-market alternative. **A personal-utility idea skips this check** — it's welcome even without monetization.

3. **The gates (the curation) — run BEFORE carding, in order; each renders in the memo, never a footnote.**

   **3.0. DEDUP GATE (do-not-repeat) — the FIRST gate, hard, re-run here even though the triage already screened.** For **each** candidate, name the **single nearest existing card** (from the EXCLUSION SET) and the **pain each addresses**. Then decide:
   - **Same core pain → it is a DUPLICATE → DROP it from the run** (not carded, not presented as new), *regardless of* a different name, angle, solution, tech, or re-scope. "A different solution to a pain already on the board is NOT a new idea." This holds across **any status** — discarded means *do not bring it back*, in-pipeline/shipped/discovered means *already here*.
   - **A genuine improvement/re-scope of an existing card → UPDATE that card** (append the new evidence/angle to it), **never create a second card** for the same pain. A re-scope is an edit, not a birth.
   - **Genuinely different pain → it passes** to 3a. The bar: you can state the existing card's pain and this candidate's pain and they are *different problems*, not the same problem dressed differently.
   Render this as a one-line-per-survivor **"dedup check: nearest card = X · distinct because <different PAIN, not different solution>"**. If you cannot name a clearly different pain, it does NOT pass. (This gate exists because a re-scope/rename leaked a duplicate onto the board — e.g. authenticator → anti-fake checklist — which is the exact "don't repeat" the owner rejects.)

   **3a. PRE-FILTER founder-fit (Paul Graham's 3 traits) — hard gate.** Does this nacer from something the owner or his niche *already live*? · can Pandacorp build it? · do few see it's worth doing? **Lens-aware:** for the **incumbent-challenger** lens, "lives the problem" is replaced by "**can he reach the first 10 users**" (pain-point SEO / communities / word-of-mouth) — the owner picked it in Phase 1, so the taste box is already ticked; what must survive is distribution. A candidate with no path to its first users is dropped (or demoted) **before** spending tokens on a memo.

   **3b. GATE painkiller / vitamin / candy** — 4 hard questions: does the pain recur (weekly / embedded in a flow)? · ROI in one sentence? · is there a prior workaround? · would they not hesitate at a monthly price? Render as an **H/M/L label with the cited evidence per question** — NEVER a meter/gauge (an agent-self-scored gauge is theater). A vitamin is not killed but its priority drops.

   **3c. GATE complexity bucket** — classify **micro / small / large** (buildability, not a day count — discover never sees the code). List the **risk accelerators** that can break the estimate (auth, anti-bot scraping, third-party approval like Meta, non-existent ground-truth, PE payments, **platform-redesign maintenance for extensions**). Default-acceptable = **micro + small**. A **large** only survives with a near-sure monetary case (recurrent pain + strong complaints + strong monetization); otherwise the memo PROPOSES the re-scope to its core, or kills it.

   **3d. GATE ground-truth + legal** — when the app emits a judgment, ask the kill question **"where does the app get the truth?"** (`ground_truth_source`: API/dataset/community/owner-as-curator/**none**). With no credible source the verdict **cannot be build** (drop to validate or re-scope: e.g. "authenticator" → expert-curated "signal checklist" that educates, never dictates). **Legal risk (owner rule): HIGH → kill; LOW → present with a disclaimer.** A generic disclaimer does NOT neutralize a high risk. For app-enhancement ideas, platform ToS hostility counts here (the map in `sources.md`): a platform that bans end-user accounts = HIGH = kill.

   **3e. Red-team kill-gate (pre-mortem).** Run every survivor through "it's 6 months later and this failed — why?" against the kill criteria, in order of how often they kill:
   1. **No distribution** — can't name the channel + the concrete first customer.
   2. **Vitamin, not painkiller** — "nice to have", not "need this week".
   3. **No observable demand** — no first-order evidence (complaints / 1-2★ reviews / searches / paid alternatives / hiring-to-do-it).
   4. **Strong well-funded incumbent without a defensible wedge** — for challenger ideas: the incumbent can copy the wedge in a sprint (a bolted-on feature is copiable; a cannibalizing price/simplicity or an AI-native flow is not).
   5. **Too complex for the window** — beyond micro/small with no near-sure monetary case, or needs data/licenses/regulation that don't exist.
   6. **Not founder-fit** — no access to users (and, outside the challenger lens, the owner doesn't live the problem).
   7. **Market = "everyone"** — can't name a specific first customer.
   8. **Doesn't work in the owner's market** (monetary ideas) — the revenue rail / customer base doesn't exist/pay from Perú. Kill-or-reframe (build-for-others, or an in-market model); kill only if money was the sole value.
   9. **Matches an owner rejection pattern** (Step 0). Kill it and name the pattern.

   A hard failure (1-4 especially) is **KILLED** — it does NOT become a card, even though the owner picked it in Phase 1 (tell him what the deep dive found; his pick bought the investigation, not a free pass). Killed ideas are listed in the report as "**descartadas y por qué**" (one line each). Auto-kills are reported, not carded (only the *owner* turns an idea into a `discarded` card, from Mission Control).

4. **Exclude — don't re-surface (enforced by Gate 3.0).** Before writing any card, re-check its pain against the EXCLUSION SET one last time under the Gate 3.0 rule: a candidate whose core pain already exists in ANY status is dropped (or, if a genuine improvement, UPDATES the existing card — you never write a second). Survivors must be **genuinely NEW PROBLEMS**.

5. **Verdict + extension check (for each survivor).**
   - **Verdict** (asymmetric): **🏆 build** · **🧪 validate** (a key unknown → a concrete 7-day test) · **🔌 integrate** — but per the owner rule, "integrate" means **propose it as an EXTENSION of the named owned app** (`fold_into`), framed as new value for that app, NOT a near-duplicate standalone and NOT silently dropped.
   - **Scorecard** — 5 axes, H/M/L: **founder-fit** · **wedge** · **esfuerzo** (micro/small/large) · **demanda** · **retorno**.
   - The conviction hooks: **why now**, **por qué tú** (anchored to his channel/niche — or, challenger lens, to the named distribution path), **#1 surviving kill-risk**, **7-day validation step**, `painkiller_class`, `ground_truth_source`, `legal_risk`, `distribution_channel` (or "none" = low priority), `prior_workaround`.

6. **Audit before rendering (the anti-hype belt).** Run **SUCCESs** (Simple · Unexpected · Concrete · **Credible** —evidence with real links— · Emotional · Story) + the **YC Email Test** on the one-liner (if a smart friend can't re-explain it without a question, rewrite it). If **Credible** fails (no real evidence), do NOT show it. Persuasion goes AFTER the curation — only sell hard what passed the hard gates; the owner is an engineer and punishes hype on weak ideas with more distrust.

7. **Document the survivors as decision-ready cards.** Curate, don't buffet: only the picked-and-surviving (usually 1-3). Cards at `factory/ideas/<slug>.md` (`_idea-template.md` format, `origin: discovery`, `status: discovered`), full frontmatter incl. the gate fields (`complexity_bucket`, `painkiller_class`, `ground_truth_source`, `legal_risk`, `prior_workaround`, `distribution_channel`) + the decision fields. Score with the SAME rubric as `new-idea` step 4 (single scale across the funnel) and record the one-line breakdown in `score_rationale:`. **The body MUST follow `_idea-template.md`'s rendering contract EXACTLY** — Mission Control's **Propuesta** tab parses these markers to render the rich memo (hero, coloured quote, feature grid + mock, red/green risk↔mitigation cards, pre-mortem & ask boxes, scorecard + chart). **Mirror the proven worked example at `plugin/templates/idea-card-example.md`** (a complete card that renders rich — copy its structure, headings and markers; colours/charts/bold are applied AUTOMATICALLY by the component from the markers, you never "paint" them). Use them verbatim (body is gitignored Spanish):
   - The lead `> **Veredicto: …** — **La apuesta:** <one-liner>` **and** a `> **Badges:** <veredicto> | cubeta: <MICRO·2-3 días|…> | <painkiller|vitamin|candy>·<descriptor> | retorno: <…>` line (PIPE-separated).
   - **`## 🔥 De un vistazo`** with `### subsections`: `### La apuesta` · `### El problema` (PAS prose **then** a real testimonial as a `> blockquote`) · `### Por qué ahora` · `### Por qué tú` (anchored to @pandadcollector / One Piece / the named channel) · `### La visión` (4 `- **Title** — desc` bullets → feature grid; optional `**Vista previa — <url>**` + `- ✓`/`- !` lines → UI mock).
   - The scorecard `| Eje | founder-fit | … |` table.
   - **`## ❄️ Profundizar`** with `### subsections`: `### Mercado` (honest SOM) · `### Distribución` (channel, or "no channel = low priority") · `### Gaps y fuente de la verdad` (the ground-truth question + a `| Riesgo | Mitigación |` table) · `### Riesgo legal` (HIGH→kill / LOW→disclaimer) · `### Red team` (pre-mortem) · `### El ask` · `### Evidencia` (≥2 real links).

   The card `.md` is the **source of truth** (state + reasoning); the Propuesta tab renders it natively. **Never hand-author bespoke HTML per idea** (that invites fabricated numbers) — write the markdown markers and let the component render.

8. **Report = a decision view** (not a catalog). Everything presented is **genuinely NEW**. Lead with **the hero** (the one to build — chosen by pain + fit + buildability, NOT return type) + a one-line why. Then the surviving picks (idea · veredicto · la apuesta · cubeta · fit-canal), then "**descartadas y por qué**" (picks killed by the deep dive, one line each), then "**extender lo que ya tienes**" (extension proposals, naming the app). Close with "*(omití N ideas que ya están en tu base, incluidas las descartadas)*". Verdict-first, scannable, ONE clear recommendation.

## Rules
- **Two phases, always.** The teaser sweep is cheap and wide; the deep dive is expensive and narrow — and only runs on the owner's picks. Never write a memo or a card for a candidate the owner hasn't picked (exception: an unattended run stops at the sheet). His pick buys the investigation, not a free pass — the gates can still kill it.
- **Sources are the verified playbook (`sources.md`).** Researchers mine only sources marked fetchable, with the tested query mechanics. Trend listicles never generate ideas. G2/Trustpilot/X-search/Google-Play are dead to WebFetch — don't burn tokens on them.
- **Lenses rotate; the collector lens is dormant.** Don't drill a well the board already saturates. Anti-picks are hard: WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Spotify-API companions, YouTube ad-blocking.
- **Curate, don't buffet.** 8-12 teasers, then only the picked; kill the rest and show why. What you DON'T show is as much of the value as what you do.
- **Hot → cold (Klaff STRONG).** The dream first (it ilusiona), the rigor after (it gives criterio). Never open with the scorecard.
- **The gates are mandatory and rendered:** dedup by PROBLEM · founder-fit (lens-aware) · painkiller H/M/L (no gauge) · complexity bucket (micro/small/large, no day count) · ground-truth + legal (high=kill, low=disclaimer; platform-ToS hostility counts). Each appears in the memo.
- **3 return paths equal priority; bias to micro/small.** A `large` only with a near-sure monetary case, else re-scope or kill. For challengers: unbundle (80/20) beats rebuild — it's the structural wedge AND the micro shape.
- **Market-reality for monetary ideas only.** A personal-utility idea (helps the owner's own life) is welcome without monetization. Perú payments per the verified table in `sources.md`.
- **Extension, not subsumption.** When a candidate overlaps an owned app, propose it as an **extension** of that app (don't freeze the app's scope, don't hide the idea, don't ship a near-duplicate standalone).
- **Never re-recommend — dedup by PROBLEM (triage + Gate 3.0).** The operational rule is Gate 3.0: a candidate whose core pain is already on the board in ANY status (incl. discarded) is excluded for the whole run. Discarding = `status: discarded`, never delete (the Descartado column IS the do-not-recommend memory). Learn from the discard REASON, not just the title; distil durable patterns into `factory/profile.md` (DR-053).
- **Learn the owner's taste.** Every pick and every pass on a teaser sheet is signal: record reactions on the sheet (`factory/ideas/_drafts/discover-*.md`), distil generalizable attraction/rejection patterns into the profile. The owner's TikTok comments/DMs are the highest-signal source the agent can't reach — when he pastes themes, treat them as seed candidates (own-itch lens; they can wake the collector lens).
- **Evidence first** (≥2 real links in a card, prefer 1-2★ reviews and past-behavior workarounds over opinion; the WTP oracle for monetary ideas); **problem as PAS storytelling**; **anti-hype** (SUCCESs Credible + Email Test gate before showing); **don't inflate** the score.
- It runs on demand today; designed to also run as a scheduled job (no human) — in that case it runs Phase 1 only, reports the teaser sheet and records it.
