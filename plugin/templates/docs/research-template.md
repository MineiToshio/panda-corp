# Research — Replace with the product title

> The evidence base behind the PRD's value hypothesis: the pain, the market, feasibility. Cite real
> sources (URLs). This informs `docs/product/prd.md` — keep the conclusions, link the digging.

## Problem & demand evidence

The pain being solved and the evidence it's real and felt (quotes, threads, search/volume signals).
Cite sources.

## Market & competitors

Who already serves this, how, and at what price. Where the gap / wedge is. For monetary/mixed: the
**price anchor** (what live paying competitors charge) that feeds the PRD's unit-economics block.

## Launch market & language (DR-041)

Which market/country to launch in first and in which language(s) — single vs multi from the MVP —
weighing market size/competition, acquisition ease, ROI and the owner's country/language (from
`factory/profile.md`). Put the variables on the table and RECOMMEND; the owner approves in the PRD.

## Existing solutions & alternatives

What people use today (incl. the "do nothing" / spreadsheet baseline) and why it falls short.

## Technical feasibility

Data sources, APIs, models or integrations the product depends on — availability, limits, cost,
licensing. The hard "can we even build this?" risks. *Omit if trivially feasible.*

## Risks & unknowns

The assumptions that, if wrong, sink the bet — and what would de-risk them.

## Verdict

The viability read that feeds the PRD: go / shape-it-differently / not now — with the one-line why.
